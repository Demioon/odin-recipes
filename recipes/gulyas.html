Description

Most famous Hungarian dish.

Ingredients

  • 60 dkg/1.3 lbs beef shank
  • 30 dkg/0.66 lbs onion
  • 2 carrot
  • 1 parsnip
  • 1 small celery
  • 40 dkg/0.88 lbs peeled potato
  • 1 tbsp sweet ground red pepper
  • 0.5 tbsp spicy ground red pepper
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 small tomato
  • 1 Hungarian pepper (tv paprika)
  • 3 clove garlic
  • a little boquet parsley
  • 2 tbsp pork lard
  • 2.5 l/33.8 oz water

Steps

  1. Dice the onions into fine pieces. Wash the meat,rinse and cut it to 2 cm cubes.
  2. Heat the lard and sautee the onions. When they are glassy, remove from the heat and add the red pepper. Mix then add 1.5 dl/5 oz water and fry until the water evaporates.
  3. At this point we can add the beef dices and sautee them until they turn white. Now you can add the salt, black pepper, cumin, crushed garlic, diced tomato, whole Hungarian pepper and the bay leaves.
  4. With the lid on, cook for 90 minutes.
  5. Now we add the vegetables: carrot and parsnip cut into circles, celery and potato cut into dices and the finely diced parsley. Add 2-2.5l/67-84 oz water
  6. Again with the lid on cook for another 50 minutes until it's done.
